## Context
The Evolution Engine includes a Reaper mechanism that deprecates
underperforming variants. If personality is subject to the Reaper, CLIVE's
identity could drift or be retired by evolutionary pressure — making it a
tool rather than a presence.

## Options Considered
Options not explicitly recorded in original decision entry.

## Decision
Personality (Block 1) survives the Reaper. CLIVE's identity is not subject
to the Evolution Engine's deprecation mechanism.

## Rationale
Continuity of identity is what makes CLIVE a presence rather than a tool.

## Consequences
Rules out the Evolution Engine targeting personality parameters. Rules out
the Reaper retiring personality variants. D-039 resolves how this is
implemented (versioned constitutional document plus system prompt content).
