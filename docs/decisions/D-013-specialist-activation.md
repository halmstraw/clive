## Context
Activating a specialist agent before the substrate is ready, or before its
block group is on the critical path, wastes context and adds coordination
overhead with no benefit.

## Options Considered
Options not explicitly recorded in original decision entry.

## Decision
New specialists are activated on the Architect's recommendation, approved
by the owner.

## Rationale
Not explicitly recorded in original entry.

## Consequences
Rules out owner-initiated activation without Architect input. Rules out
automatic activation. Every specialist activation is a deliberate,
recorded step.
