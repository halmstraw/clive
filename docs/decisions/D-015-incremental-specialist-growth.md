## Context
Activating all seven specialists at the start would create maximum
coordination overhead before any block is operational. Most specialists
cannot contribute meaningfully until the substrate exists.

## Options Considered
Options not explicitly recorded in original decision entry.

## Decision
The specialist agent team grows incrementally as the build requires,
not all at once.

## Rationale
Not explicitly recorded in original entry.

## Consequences
Rules out upfront instantiation of all seven specialists. Each activation
follows D-013 (Architect recommendation, owner approval).

## Related Decisions
D-013 (activation process), D-016 (parallel build within active
specialists), D-019 (Systems and Infrastructure Agents activated first).
